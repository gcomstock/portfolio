## Problem Definition

import imgProbBestPractices from './images/no-best-practices.png';
import imgProbAccountability from './images/no-accountability.png';

<ColGrid>
  <ColGridItem image={imgProbBestPractices} contain title="No Best Practices">
    <p>Want to start a new Java or React app? You could look and ask around for advice, but teams were left to their own devices to come up with their own solutions. Needed to create a source of truth for discovering best practices for software development at Netflix. This was especially important for new hires who had little assistance ramping up and understanding the internal tool chain.</p>
  </ColGridItem>
  <ColGridItem image={imgProbAccountability} contain title="No Accountability">
    <p>Code at Netflix is not a mono repo; it's a spiderweb of thousands of micro-services, each with their own deployment and support schedules. As engineers left the company and teams re-org'd, touching dependencies felt like walking a minefield. Some less active services had unknown owners and were left to run until something broke.</p>
  </ColGridItem>
  <ColGridItem image={imgProbStartingPlace} contain title="No Starting Place">
    <p>Where do users go to deploy software, or see resiliency trends? How about observing production performance, or alerting and troubleshooting incidents? Every stop in the SDLC had its own cleverly-named product that had few or no connection points with others.</p>
  </ColGridItem>
</ColGrid>

### Finding Our Starting Point

<ColGrid>
  <ColGridItem image={imgArchProductivity} contain transparent title="Platform and Product">
    <p>It was clear that our small team would be unable to implement every feature for every team within developer productivity at Netflix. We needed to define the scaffolding and design standards so that partner teams could follow our patterns and maintain their own plugins.</p>
  </ColGridItem>
  <ColGridItem image={imgArchBackstage} contain transparent title="Working with Spotify">
    <p>Backstage, being open source, was able to accelerate our front-end development efforts, particularly around interfacing with a software catalog.</p>
  </ColGridItem>
  <ColGridItem image={imgArchHawkins} contain transparent title="Internal Design System">
    <p>We chose to use our own design system over Backstage's out of the box components, so partner teams that were integrating with their existing experiences had the same visuals and UX patterns as Console itself.</p>
  </ColGridItem>
</ColGrid>

</GradientBlock>
